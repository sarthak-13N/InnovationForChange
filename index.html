About Us

Welcome to E-Cell YMC! Founded on August 24, 2024, by passionate students Sarthak Nimble and Tejas Sanjay Barguje, our entrepreneurial cell is dedicated to fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among the youth at Yashavantrao Mohite College.

At E-Cell YMC, we believe in the power of ideas and the spirit of collaboration. Our mission is to create a vibrant ecosystem where aspiring entrepreneurs can connect, learn, and grow. Through workshops, networking events, and mentorship programs, we aim to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to turn their entrepreneurial dreams into reality.

Join us in our journey to inspire and empower the next generation of leaders and innovators. Together, we can make a difference!
